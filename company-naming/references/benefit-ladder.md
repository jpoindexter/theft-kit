# Benefit Ladder

Great names usually come from what the company means, not just what it does.

## Ladder Structure

Move upward through four layers:

1. Feature
2. Functional benefit
3. Emotional benefit
4. Ultimate benefit

## Example

For a project-management product:

- Feature: task tracking and team coordination
- Functional benefit: fewer missed deadlines and less chaos
- Emotional benefit: feeling in control
- Ultimate benefit: clarity

The naming territory becomes much richer at "clarity" than at "project management."

## Output

Write:

- the current feature-level description
- the emotional or identity shift the customer wants
- the 1 to 2-word ultimate-benefit territory

Use that top-level concept as the main springboard for name generation.
