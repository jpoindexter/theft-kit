# Finalist Scorecard

Use this scorecard to compare finalists once the list is narrow enough to judge.

## Criteria

Score each 1 to 10:

- Distinctiveness: Does it stand apart from competitors?
- Processing fluency: Is it easy to say, spell, and remember?
- Emotional resonance: Does it connect to the desired feeling or benefit?
- Energy: Does it create a reaction instead of fading into the background?
- Longevity: Can it still work if the company broadens or scales?

## Tension Test

After scoring, ask:

- Which option has the most energy?
- Which option risks being invisible?
- Which option sounds like the company's ambition, not just today's feature set?

Do not let a merely balanced name beat a remarkable one by default.

## Practical Checks

Add a note for:

- domain possibilities
- social handle plausibility
- trademark follow-up needs

These are screening notes, not final legal clearance unless a dedicated legal review has happened.
