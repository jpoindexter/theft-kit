# Landscape And Brief

Start naming by defining the territory before you generate names.

## Inputs To Gather

- what the company does
- who it serves
- the desired feeling or impression
- the category it competes in
- main competitors or adjacent alternatives
- names the team likes and dislikes

## Landscape Pass

List 10 to 20 relevant names if possible, then identify:

- dominant patterns in the category
- repeated suffixes or fragments
- common literal words
- whether the category skews descriptive, metaphorical, invented, or acronym-heavy

Then write a short "not going there" summary:

- overused category terms
- obvious descriptive constructions
- naming patterns that would make the company hard to distinguish

## Naming Brief Output

Capture:

- company summary
- target customer
- desired brand feeling
- off-limits territories
- naming constraints
- early hypotheses about promising territory
